about us

The Honey Festival is where indie and psychedelic culture collide, bringing together music, art, and community in a laid-back, vibrant setting. With 24/7 activities, including workshops, panels, and live performances, the festival offers something for everyone. Whether you're discovering new music, relaxing in our chill zones, or immersing yourself in unique experiences, the Honey Festival promises to be an unforgettable journey. Come as you are and leave with memories that last a lifetime.

where you can find us

location

Located in the heart of Big Bear, our festival site pulsates with energy and creativity. Follow the rhythmic beats and melodious tunes as they guide you through the lively streets, leading you to our festival haven. Nestled in Stanfield Marsh Wildlife Boardwalk Park, the air is filled with the aroma of delicious street food and the sights of colorful art installations. Dance under the open sky, surrounded by enthusiastic music lovers, as the sun sets and the stars illuminate the night.

let's talk

FAQs

What is Honey Festival?

Honey Festival is a four-day indie music festival featuring live concerts, DJ sets, workshops, panels, and more. A celebration of music, art, and community.

What kind of music can I expect?

The festival features a mix of indie, psychedelic, folk, and alternative artists, along with immersive DJ sets with popular DJs. Make sure to check the line up.

Where can I buy tickets?

Tickets can be purchased online. Be sure to grab them early - prices increase closer to the event! There will also be a limited number of tickets at the gate.

Are there age restrictions?

Honey Festival is an 18+ event. Attendees must bring a valid photo ID for entry. Attendees under 18 must be accompanied by an adult.

Can I camp at the festival?

Yes! We have many options. A designated camping area, plus glamping options for those who want extra comfort.

What items are prohibited?

Prohibited items include weapons, illegal substances, glass bottles, large speakers, and outside alcohol. A full list of banned items will be provided before the festival.

How can I get involved?

You can apply to volunteer for free entry or to be a vendor or sponsor as well as be an ambassador.

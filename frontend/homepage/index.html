what you should know

about us

The Honey Festival is where indie and psychedelic culture collide, bringing together music, art, and community in a laid-back, vibrant setting. With 24/7 activities, including workshops, panels, and live performances, the festival offers something for everyone. Whether you're discovering new music, relaxing in our chill zones, or immersing yourself in unique experiences, the Honey Festival promises to be an unforgettable journey. Come as you are and leave with memories that last a lifetime.

where you can find us

location

Located in the heart of Big Bear, our festival site pulsates with energy and creativity. Follow the rhythmic beats and melodious tunes as they guide you through the lively streets, leading you to our festival haven. Nestled in Stanfield Marsh Wildlife Boardwalk Park, the air is filled with the aroma of delicious street food and the sights of colorful art installations. Dance under the open sky, surrounded by enthusiastic music lovers, as the sun sets and the stars illuminate the night.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26th, 2025



What you need to know

Introduction

Welcome to Honey Festival! This privacy policy applies to Honey's website and all related websites, software, mobile apps, plug-ins and other services that we provide (together, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or participate in our festival. It also explains how you can exercise your privacy rights. If you have any questions, suggestions or complaints about our use of your personal information or this privacy policy, please contact us using the details provided at the bottom of this page.



Collecting info

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and payment details when purchasing tickets or signing up for our newsletter.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, and website usage data to improve our services.


Using info

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process ticket purchases and event registrations.
  • Send festival updates, promotions, and newsletters (you can opt out anytime).
  • Improve our website and festival experience.
  • Ensure safety and security at our event.


Info share

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share it with:

  • Trusted third-party vendors (e.g., payment processors, ticketing services) to fulfill your purchases.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.


Protecting data

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no online transmission is 100% secure, so we encourage you to take precautions.



Cookies

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.



User rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing emails.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.


Third-party

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.



Updating

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.



Contacting

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at honey@honey.com.



By using our website or attending our festival, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

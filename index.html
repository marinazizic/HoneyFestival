what you should know

about us

Honey is a vibrant celebration of independent art and music, bringing together talented artists and passionate enthusiasts in a one-of-a-kind experience. Join us as we embrace the spirit of creativity, push boundaries, showcase the best of indie talent, and where the energy of indie artistry comes alive. Welcome to Honey, where passion, innovation, and artistic freedom take center stage.

where you can find us

location

Located in the heart of Beverly Hills, our festival site pulsates with energy and creativity. Follow the rhythmic beats and melodious tunes as they guide you through the lively streets, leading you to our festival haven. Nestled in Beverly Gardens Park, the air is filled with the aroma of delicious street food and the sights of colorful art installations. Dance under the open sky, surrounded by enthusiastic music lovers, as the sun sets and the stars illuminate the night.
